Services need to be...

In a distributed system, there are many interactions between services. Services need to be discoverable and easily (but securely) accessible. These interactions must be efficient and fault tolerant.

Stateless services are constantly...

scaling

failing

recovering

The ephemeral nature of stateless services coupled with the elastic scalability of the cloud, make service interactions a challenging problem.

In distributed systems...

Application dependencies cease to be a method call away

Configuring each client is error prone and brittle

In a monolith, the dependencies are all there or the monolith is completely down.

Service Discovery

Client Side load-balancing

Service discovery leverages a fault tolerant, distributed and scalable registry. The registry allows services to self register and client to find instances of services. Client side load-balancing allows clients to directly connect, removing the single point of failure in many ESB solutions.

Client side load-balancing is...

Simple - no need for another load-balancer

Efficient - minimize components between calls

Flexible - URIs change

Client’s should be able to access services directly without knowing brittle credentials like uris. Load balancers fail. Instances come and go.
